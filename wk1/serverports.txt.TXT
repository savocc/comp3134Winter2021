• What is the definition of server port?
Answer: A port is a logical construct that represents a proccess of network service. 
A port is identified for each transport protocol and address combination by a 16-bit unsigned number, known as the port number.



• What is server port range?
Answer: Registered ports are in the range 1024 to 49151. 
Dynamic ports are in the range 49152 to 65535.
Most new port assignments are in the range from 1024 to 49151. 



• How many bits does number above represent?
Answer: A 16-bit integer can store 2^16 (or 65,536) distinct values.
